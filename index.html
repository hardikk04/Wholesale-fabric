J Stimler is a good quality fabric design and bulk production service for fashion retailers around the world.

A wholesale fabric supplier, with a talented in-house team of experts, who create original trend-setting, commercial print designs, based out of our studio in Stoke Newington, London.

All of the fabrics we supply are manufactured using good standard materials, and following good standard manufacturing processes.

J Stimler is committed to investing more into sustainable options.

There is no doubt that the future of fashion is green. More and more retailers are adopting using part/only sustainable fabric.
